Data Science Roadmap
 A structured guide to entering and excelling in the data-driven world
Introduction
Data science sits at the intersection of mathematics, statistics, programming, and domain expertise. In 2026, it
remains one of the most in-demand career paths globally, powering decisions across healthcare, finance,
e-commerce, and beyond. This roadmap provides a structured, professional path from zero to job-ready data
scientist.
